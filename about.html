About Maplebrook Public Library

Maplebrook Public Library has been serving our community for over 60 years. From books and digital media to programs for all ages, MPL is a welcoming space designed to inspire curiosity, creativity, and lifelong learning.

Our branches offer a wide range of free resources, study spaces, events, and community services—ensuring everyone in Maplebrook has access to knowledge and opportunities.

Whether you're here to borrow a book, attend a workshop, or simply relax in a quiet corner, MPL is your home for discovery.

Our Mission & Values

We believe in empowering every member of our community through accessible knowledge, inclusive spaces, and meaningful connections.

Community First

MPL is built around people—creating safe, supportive, and enriching environments for all ages and backgrounds.

Accessible for Everyone

From barrier-free building design to digital resources, MPL strives to make learning accessible to everyone.

Growing Together

We’re dedicated to lifelong learning and collaborative growth through workshops, programs, and community partnerships.
